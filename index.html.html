About

Most high-performing executives have the capability for C-suite leadership—but lack the positioning that makes decision-makers see them as ready.

I partner with ambitious VPs and SVPs who are ready for C-suite roles but need strategic positioning to make it happen. Through deep-dive interviewing and candid coaching, I help translate 15-25 year career trajectories into compelling narratives that open doors at Fortune 500 organizations, PE-backed firms, and high-growth technology companies.

This isn't just resume writing. It's strategic advisory for executives who understand that the path to the C-suite requires more than credentials—it requires positioning, timing, and the right relationships.

About August

I hold an MS in Operations Management and previously sold complex B2B financial products including commercial premium financing services and import/export trade credit insurance to CXOs at med/large and global Fortune companies. That experience shaped how I work today; I understand how senior leaders think, how decisions get made at the top, and what it takes to be seen as ready for the next level.

For the last 18 years, I've advised 650+ senior executives through career transitions, promotions, and pivots. My approach is direct: deep-dive interviews that surface what makes you distinctive, candid feedback that challenges assumptions, and strategic positioning that opens doors. Clients tell me I'm a straight talker—I take that as a compliment.
